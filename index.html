A few Facts about us

Who we are and what we do

Rising Educational Consultancy is a private institution that deals with providing the right solutions for your educational works, businesses, abroad scholarships,tertiary admissions, birth certification and passports. Our Services include:

  • Scholarships: We assists students for study abroad scholarships (Fully funded) and some Ghanaian scholarships.
  • Admission forms: Selling of tertiary admission forms (Vourchers) and assistance in its application for fast admissions
  • Results Checkers: Sales of WASSCE, NOV/DEC, BECE, SSCE, GBCE and ABCE results checkers available in bulk purchase
  • Assistance: We provide assistance in educational guidance and counseling, registration and preparation for TOEFL, SAT, IELTS and GRE.
  • Birth Cert and Passports: We help in legit, easy, and fast issuing of your Birth cert or Passport ranging from 1 week to 3 months at affordable prices.
